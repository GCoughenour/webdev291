About Me

After looking into the Web Development and Design degree I felt I could let my creativity flow a little more freely. So I decided to switch my major to Web Development and Design. Now I really enjoy my professors, classes, and most importantly, my work. Now when I look at my assignments and other things I have done I have pride in being able to say "I made that!" and I am proud of myself.
